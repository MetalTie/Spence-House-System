House System

Four Houses One Family

The house system is a dynamic, exciting, and proven way to create a positive climate and culture for students and staff. Using RCA’s methods will help Spence confidently implement processes that build character, relationships, and school spirit. The house system is designed to give us a streamlined framework that can be applied to any type of learning environment.

The House System At Spence Elementary

  • Build school wide community
  • Character building
  • Culture of belonging
  • Healthy competition
  • Encourage unity amongst ALL

House Goals

  • Mission: Spence Elementary is committed to educating all students by fostering a safe and positive learning environment where every student will grow and develop academically, socially, and emotionally.
  • Vision: Spence Elementary will provide rigorous, learner centered instruction to maximize students’ potentials to exceed their goals through using the core values of PRIDE (Perseverance, Responsibility, Integrity, Determination, and Excellence).
